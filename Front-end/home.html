Human Pathogen Sequence Repository

The purpose of HuPat is to consolidate the genomic sequencing data for human pathogenic orgnaisms. This includes Bacteria, Fungi, Protozoa, and Viruses. By creating this resource, we hope to improve the in silico screening process for assays such as molecular diagnostic detection and drug screening.

To utilze this database, enter an organism name into the search bar on the left. Data pertaining to the organism's name, rank, lineage, NCBI Taxonomy ID, and recent PubMed Articles will be displayed. Additionally a link to download the most update RefSeq assembly will also be included.
